Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard for portable restroom cleanliness?
The standard for portable restroom cleanliness includes using a high-pressure water system to thoroughly clean and disinfect surfaces, changing out the sanitation tank regularly, and thoroughly sanitizing all contact points between uses.
How often should waste rentals be serviced?
Waste rentals should be serviced on a regular basis depending on usage and the number of people using them. Generally speaking, it is recommended that they are serviced at least once every two weeks or after any large event.
What safety measures should I take when servicing waste rentals?
Safety measures for servicing waste rentals include wearing personal protective equipment such as gloves, masks, and long sleeves; ensuring adequate ventilation in enclosed areas; avoiding contact with bodily fluids; disposing of hazardous materials properly; and following all applicable safety regulations.
