Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of having a handwashing station with a portable restroom?
Handwashing stations provide users with access to a reliable source of clean water for washing their hands, which helps reduce the spread of germs and keeps users safe from potential illnesses.
How do I know if my event needs additional handwashing stations?
Most events will benefit from having at least one handwashing station per 50-100 guests as recommended by public health officials. Consider factors such as the size of your event, activities taking place, and duration when determining how many handwashing stations you may need.
Are there any special considerations for installing a handwashing station near a portable restroom?
Yes, it is important to ensure that the area where you install the handwashing station is level and free from debris or obstacles that could affect its proper functioning. It should also be close enough to the portable restroom so that people can easily access it while using the facilities.
What kind of maintenance do these units require?
To ensure proper functioning, most handwashing stations require regular cleaning and maintenance including refilling soap and water supplies, emptying wastewater tanks, checking water pressure levels, and testing temperature levels in heated models.
Are there any safety concerns that I should be aware of when renting a handwashing station?
Before use, check to make sure that all parts are properly secured and in good condition; this includes checking for exposed wiring or sharp edges on metal components which could potentially cause injury or infection if left unchecked.
