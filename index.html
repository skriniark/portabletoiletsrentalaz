What is the typical cost of a portable restroom rental?
The average cost for renting a single portable restroom typically ranges from $100 to $200 per day, depending on the type and number of units rented.
How many portable restrooms should I rent?
This depends on the size of the event, number of expected attendees, and duration of the event. Generally speaking, it is recommended to rent 1 unit for every 50 guests or 10 hours of use.
When should I reserve my porta potties?
It is best to reserve your porta potties at least two weeks in advance as this allows time to get quotes and arrange delivery and pick-up dates with your supplier.
